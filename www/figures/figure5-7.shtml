Figure 5.7 The Hox gene superfamily. Illustration of the four mouse homeobox gene clusters and the relationship of genes within each cluster to those in other clusters and Drosophila homologs in the HOM-C cluster (McGinnis and Krumlauf, 1992). Related genes from different clusters, so-called paralogous subgroups, are shown together in the same shaded region. The general spatial pattern of expression of each gene along the anterior-posterior axis within the developing embryo is indicated below. This illustration was kindly provided by Robert Krumlauf (Mill Hill, England).
