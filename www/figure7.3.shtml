Figure 7.3 Non-independent assortment of alleles from linked loci. Illustration of a parent heterozygous for alternative alleles at the linked A and B loci, and the four classes of meiotic products that it will generate. As a consequence of linkage, the parental combinations of alleles will be transmitted to gametes at a higher frequency than the recombinant combinations. In this particular example, the A and B loci show recombination at a frequency of 10%.
