Figure D1 No recombination between loci in 50 backcross animals. The probability density function is plotted for interlocus distances associated with the experimental finding of no recombination between two loci within a group of 50 backcross animals. This function is defined by the equation f(small-theta) = (1 - small-theta) 50 in which small-theta is the recombination fraction. Recombination fractions are converted into centimorgan distances ( d-bar ) through the assumption of complete interference over the range shown. Minimum and maximum confidence limits are indicated for the 68% interval and the 95% interval. The median linkage distance estimate d-bar is defined at the midline of the area under the complete density function.
